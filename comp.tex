\begin{enumerate}
	\item Standard consistency conditions:\\
	1. Identification: $lim_{n\rightarrow \inf}Q_n(\theta) = Q_0(\theta)$. $Q_0(\theta)$ exists and is only maximized at $\theta = \theta 0$.\\
	2. Smoothness: $Q_n(\theta)$ is differentiable in $\theta$.\\
	3. Compactness: $\Theta$ is a compact set.\\
	4. Stochastic-equicontinuity: a sufficient condition for this is \[sup_{\theta \in \Theta}|\frac{\partial Q_n(\theta)}{\partial\theta}|\] is bounded in probability.
	\item Invariance property of the maximum likelihood estimator:\\
	It requires $g(\theta)$ to be bounded, continuous and does not depend on sample size n.
	\item Delta method\\
	It requires $\frac{dg(a)}{da'}$ to be finite and non-zero, plus not depending on n and continuously differentiable in a neighborhood of $a$.

\end{enumerate}
